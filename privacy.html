Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What this site collects

This site does not require an account, and does not ask you to submit any personal information. All filter choices (mode, tonic, position, language) are stored only in your own browser's local storage so they're remembered between visits — that data never leaves your device.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand approximate visitor numbers and which pages are popular. Google Analytics may use cookies and collect standard web analytics data (such as approximate location, device/browser type, and pages visited). It does not collect names, emails, or any other personally identifying information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using browser extensions such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising

This site does not currently run any ads. If ad placements are added in the future, this policy will be updated to describe what data, if any, the ad provider collects.

Contact

Questions or feedback about this policy or the site? Please open an issue on GitHub Issues.
